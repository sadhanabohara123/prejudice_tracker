About Prejudice Tracker

Prejudice Tracker is a groundbreaking initiative aimed at monitoring and combating prejudice and discrimination in our communities. Developed by a team of dedicated individuals passionate about social justice, the Prejudice Tracker platform serves as a vital tool in raising awareness, fostering dialogue, and promoting inclusivity.

Join Us

At Prejudice Tracker, our mission is simple yet profound: to shine a light on prejudice wherever it exists and empower individuals and communities to take meaningful action against it. We believe that by identifying and confronting prejudice head-on, we can build a more equitable and compassionate society for all.
Through innovative data collection methods and advanced analytics, Prejudice Tracker gathers and analyzes information on incidents of prejudice and discrimination across various contexts, including but not limited to race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, and disability. Our platform aggregates these reports to provide insights into patterns, trends, and hotspots of prejudice, helping to inform policymakers, educators, activists, and the general public.
Prejudice Tracker is a community-driven project, and your participation is crucial to its success. Whether you've experienced prejudice firsthand or witnessed it in your community, we encourage you to report incidents through our user-friendly interface. By sharing your story, you contribute to a collective effort to combat prejudice and promote understanding.
Together, we can make a difference. Join the Prejudice Tracker community today and be part of the movement to create a more inclusive and equitable world for everyone.

Our Services

Report an Incident

If you've experienced or witnessed an incident of prejudice or discrimination, we encourage you to report it by clicking on 'Report an Incident' button below.

Before reporting an incident, gather essential details: time, location, and involved parties. Clearly articulate the incident's impact and potential consequences to ensure a swift and effective response.

View Reported Incidents

Presenting a comprehensive list of previously reported incidents. From minor hiccups to significant disruptions, each is documented for transparency and analysis. Gain valuable insights into past occurrences to inform decision-making and process refinement. Explore diverse scenarios, each offering unique lessons as we pursue operational excellence and resilience.

Empowering Education on Prejudice and Discrimination

At Prejudice Tracker, we believe that education is a powerful tool in combating prejudice and discrimination. By increasing awareness, understanding, and empathy, we can work together to create a more inclusive and equitable society.
